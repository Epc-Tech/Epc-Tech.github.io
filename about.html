About Me

I've been passionate about PC building and tech repairs since I was young. What started as a hobby quickly turned into something more when I realized how overpriced pre-built systems were. I wanted to offer affordable, high-quality, and customized solutions for people who need a reliable PC that fits their exact needs.

Unlike big retailers, I work closely with my customers to ensure they get a personalized experience. Whether you're new to PCs or a tech enthusiast, I'm here to provide expert guidance, honest pricing, and hands-on support for your system. Let’s build something great together!

Warranty & Support

I offer a two-year general warranty on every PC I build, ensuring that if anything goes wrong within that time, I will take care of it. Every component also comes with its own manufacturer warranty, and if a part like your RAM, CPU, or GPU has extended coverage (up to 5 years in some cases), I will personally assist in navigating the warranty process.

My commitment doesn’t stop when my own warranty ends. If something happens, I will check with the **manufacturer or retailer** to see if you’re still eligible for a replacement or repair. No unnecessary complications—just honest and reliable support to ensure your system runs smoothly.
